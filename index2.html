Honkai: Star Rail

Breakdown: Skytrain is a galactic adventure strategy game developed by Shanghai MiHoYo Technology .and is the fifth work in Mihayou's "Breakdown" series.
In the game, players will board the star Sky train, visit the Viviane world living in the universe, join hands with all kinds of travel companions, rescue the "star core" caused by the crisis, against the "star God" trampled on the intent of civilization.
