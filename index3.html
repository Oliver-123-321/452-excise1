Government Spending 

 The table below shows the proportion of total government spending on 
 social welfare, health, and education over the years 2011 to 2013.

year social health education
2011 0.22 0.14 0.12
2012 0.24 0.15 0.13
2013 0.25 0.16 0.14

These values suggest that, over time, the government is spending more  
of its budget in each of these three areas.

 Source: The New Zealand Treasury.
